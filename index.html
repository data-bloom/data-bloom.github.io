Sample Solutions

Data Ingestion

Do not let the raw data hinder your analytical speed. Quickly ingest, infer, explore, validate, shape and align data for insights. Mix and match your imperative, declarative, and functional programming skills on the big data platform for grappling with structural inconsistencies of your data.

Self-Service BI

Business needs quick insights. Drawing business intelligence from raw data should not be a laborious task. Stop the data engineering and BI development circus. Go from raw data to rich interactive BI in under 10 minutes.

Statistical Assumptions

Do you know that central limit theorem is pivotal to statistical (and consequently data science) theory? Do not let the parametric assumptions or non-stationarity of your data invalidate your sound science. Build implicit statistical intelligence in your data lake.

Operational Intelligence

You cannot improve for what you cannot measure. It is very important to deploy near realtime data sensors and render intelligence via dashboards so you are not caught off-guard when business systems recline in the wrong direction.

Fraud Detection

Protect your business against fraudulent activity. You want your business to have a steady operational hum but keep the bad actors away. Build self-healing capabilities into your application by giving it the intelligence to detect normative hum from anamolous noise.

Human Activity Recognition

Computing is getting extremely personal. The smartphone, since 2006, has become our constant cohort in every step of our life. How exactly does your smartphone recognize your activity and whereabouts with such precision?

Fuzzy Matching

Data issues happen. When your business relies on having clean data to deliver business excellence, it is imperative to curate, consolidate, and link data into master record-sets. Scalably deduplicate and cluster raw records to guarantee data quality in your lake.

Heuristic Routing

Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP) is a NP complete problem. Yet, in many daily activities, we want to minimize the circuitous paths and achieve optimal outcomes. When statistical accuracy can be a challenge in near realtime situations, adopt heuristic measures to achieve near-optimal outcomes.

Churn Analysis

You worked hard to build loyal employees. Do not risk losing strong performers. Gain foresight into causes for employee churn and take preemptive measures to save your A players.

Information Extraction

Much of our knowledge is captured in plain text. How do we structurally, semantically, and linguistically recode this knowledge into factual triples for best knowledge outcomes? Encode your knowledge bases for both androids and anthropoids.

Factometer

Help your information customers quickly reach objective "facts ". Do not serve humongous pages of text; analyze, extract, and plant visual markers on your content automatically so your consumers can exercise both perceptual and cognitive wisdom effectively.

Time Series Forecasting

Gain foresight into future from past performance. Embrace simple extrapolative techniques like ANOVA and ARIMA to predict and optimize pricing, supply, and demand equations.

Data Curation & Linking

Automatically collect, curate, and collate raw data through your enterprise data hierarchy

Data is the new oil. But hoarding raw data without transforming it to the enterprise standard is unproductive.

Data Bloom enables unstructured fuzzy quadratic joins so the data lake can be used to curate raw data and identify latent correlations. Employ Data Bloom to continuously process raw data, mine patterns, link entities, derive business ontologies in a mixed-initiative environment.

Personalized Search Analytics

Deliver superior outcomes for your customers with personalized search

Text search yields good results. But superior results go beyond simple search by combining explicit (stated), implicit (implied), and tacit (observed) intelligence. Each user, context, content, goals are different. Offering same results for diverse personas will indispensably disappoint customers.

Demystifying user's complex knowledge goals is very challenging, but user's digital fingerprints can be handy to infer more than is apparent from a simple search query. Tracking-pixel instrumentation is necessary for capturing this intelligence. Mining these homing signals of personalization must be a critical operational component of every search/knowledge enterprise.
